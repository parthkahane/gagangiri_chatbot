A Dream That Became a Legacy.

In 2008, a humble dream began flowing from the heart of Pimpri Chinchwad, Maharashtra. Founded by Late Mahadev Rayba Aher under the name "Gagangiri Aqua," we set out to offer something pure — not just water, but trust.

Over the years, Gagangiri Foods has evolved from a local venture to a trusted household name across Maharashtra, supplying to Pimpri Chinchwad, Pune and Lonavala region. Our unwavering commitment to quality and innovation is our true hallmark.

From the initial days of manual bottling to today's fully automated 10,000 sq.ft facility in Ganesh Nagar, Talawade, our journey is a tribute to perseverance, innovation, and generational pride. Led by the vision of Mr. Mahadev Ranba Aher and today carried forward by Mr. Anil Ranba Aher and Mr. Shripad Anil Aher, we continue to bottle purity with every drop.

Every sip you take connects you to our story — a story of hard work, dreams, and uncompromised quality.
